06 · FAQ

Common questions

What kinds of jobs do you take?
We focus on door signs, wayfinding systems, and minor finishing work related to signage changes. Jobs can be as small as a single nameplate or as large as a full office wayfinding system.
Do you help with design as well?
Yes. We can work from your existing brand guidelines or help you choose appropriate styles, materials, and layouts for your space.
Do you work on small jobs?
Absolutely. A single door sign or a small directional update is well within our scope. We don't have minimum job sizes.
Can you update existing signage?
Yes. We can replace, refresh, or reconfigure existing signage to match updated branding, tenancy changes, or new naming conventions.
Do you handle minor wall touch-ups after sign removal?
Yes. We include light patching, surface preparation, and paint touch-up as part of sign removal and replacement projects. This is finishing work — making the wall look like nothing was ever there.
Which areas do you service?
We work across Melbourne metropolitan areas. For projects outside the metro area, please ask and we'll let you know if we can accommodate.
